Leadership

Board of Directors

Guided by passion, driven by purpose. Our board brings together experienced leaders committed to creating equity and opportunity in every community we serve.

Tiffany Davidson

Executive Director & Board Chair

A passionate educator and social entrepreneur, Tiffany brings over a decade of experience in education, literacy, and workforce development. With dual Master's degrees in Literacy/Secondary Language Acquisition (TESOL) and Curriculum & Instruction, and ongoing graduate studies in Educational Administration at the University of Dayton, she founded W.I.S.E.C.E.O. to bridge the digital divide and create pathways to economic mobility for underserved communities.

David Crawford

Board Vice Chair

David brings strategic leadership and a deep commitment to community empowerment. With extensive experience in organizational development and governance, he ensures W.I.S.E.C.E.O.'s mission is reflected in every decision. His dedication to equity and excellence strengthens the organization's foundation and expands its reach.
