Our Story

Excellence is not a privilege.
It's a right.

W.I.S.E.C.E.O. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to dismantling systemic barriers that prevent underserved communities from accessing technology, education, and economic opportunity. We serve first-generation learners, immigrants, refugees, and low-income youth and adults — communities historically underrepresented in technology and the workforce.

Through coding education, digital literacy, career pathway development, and entrepreneurship support, we equip individuals with the critical skills needed to succeed in today's innovation economy — generating measurable, lasting economic impact.
